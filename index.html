International Visitor Arrivals to the United States


Welcome to the United States


This airport sign carries diverse meanings.
For some, it evokes a sense of homecoming.
While for others, it represents a doorway to new adventures.


Have you ever wondered how many international guests we welcome every year?
As you can see from the chart, the number of visitors has been on the rise.
But it is also greatly affected by significant global events.


The bubble charts illustrate the relative size of guests
from each foreign country, color-coded by their region, in 2000, 2019, and 2022.
What are the biggest changes you can observe?


Let's take a look at the time series trend for each country.
Below, you can see the trend of Top 20 countries (based on the 2019 ranking).


Which countries show remarkable growth over the last 20 years?
Which countries have plateaued or experienced a slight decline?
Which countries picked up quickly after the pandemic?


North America has always been a safe bet.
China's exponential curve is conspicuous.
However, in general, Asian countries stagnated after COVID-19 erupted.
Japan and the UK are very interesting,
but it makes sense considering their lackluster GDP growth.
